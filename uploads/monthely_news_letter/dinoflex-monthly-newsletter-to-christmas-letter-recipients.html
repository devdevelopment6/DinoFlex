DinoFall System



Developing solution-driven innovations is synonymous with our brand, and our new DinoFall System is the latest of our quality products.

Keeping the safety of your children paramount, the DinoFall System helps to further protect your climbing and playground-scrambling kids by creating greater fall heights while they play. This new 39 1/16" x 90 9/16" system is installed under any one of our PlayTiles to increase the fall height, giving both kids and parents some peace of mind should they tumble or have an accident. By varying the thickness that you choose, fall heights can range from 4’ up to 12’ and therefore can be safely matched to the structure of play. With the launch of this latest system, we will be discontinuing our 3 ½” PlayTile, allowing the DinoFall to truly let kids be kids even more.

Seaton Hays Wellness Center
Austin, Texas
Do you have a specific colour in mind for your establishment that you don't see as part of our standard colour list? For us at Dinoflex, that's not an issue and you'll be floored (pun intended!) with the variety of colours we can create to complement your brand and design space.  

Our Colour Innovator allows you to customize our recycled rubber floors to suit your design palette-it is the blank canvas of floor design and you are the artist! Ascension Seton Hays Hospital in Kyle, Texas, was a recent client of ours. As a destination for specialty care which includes heart and vascular health, cancer care, orthopedics, women's health and maternity services, they were looking for the right shades and colours in their design to deliver comfort and ease to their patients. Just like the hospital care teams listen to the needs of their patients and delivers care that's the right fit, Dinoflex helped them create their own custom products that their hospital rooms needed. Ascension Seton Hays Hospital created its own custom colour using Next Step Walk Soft – 8mm with the help of Polkinghorn Group Architects, Amy Marshall and flooring contractor Corporate Floors. Visit our website to read more!
