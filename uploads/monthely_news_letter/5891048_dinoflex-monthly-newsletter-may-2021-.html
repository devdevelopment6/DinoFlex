Sport Mat
Our Sport Mat fitness flooring is there to support health and recreation at every level. In workout environments, this flooring can handle the impact of weights and machinery, as well as reduce the risk of injury with its high density and shock-absorbing features.  

The high traction of Dinoflex recycled rubber makes this product perfect for a multitude of areas in both winter and summer sports facilities. You can count on this durable product to not show any signs of wear and is #GuaranteedNeverToCurl.

Springbank Park for All Seasons

Springbank Park for All Seasons approached Dinoflex when they needed a flooring surface that was durable, long-lasting and skate-resistant for their two ice arenas and lobby area. Dinoflex Sport Mat tiles were placed throughout their facility, including the lobby, their arenas, curling rink, cafeteria, locker rooms, fitness rooms, corridors, and offices. Springbank Park for All Seasons also opted to customize their flooring by having us add in their logo, inlaid with custom vibrant colours depicting the four seasons of their brand. 

For being such a high-traffic facility subjected to years of foot traffic, sporting equipment and ice skates, we're proud that their flooring doesn't look a day over the first day we installed it. Click below to learn more.

Did you know that our recycled rubber flooring needs a minimum of 48-72 hours of acclimation before beginning your installation? We also need to ensure that your subsurface flooring has reached a temperature between 14C (57F) and 23C (72F) prior to having your tiles installed. (This is done to account for the initial and inherent expansion and contraction of rubber products).
