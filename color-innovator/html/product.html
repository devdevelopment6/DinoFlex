Custom Design

Our precision water cutter technology enables us to create rubber floors that are both functional and visually appealing. Utilize valuable floor space by displaying company logos or brand elements repeatedly throughout a facility. Write a message to customers in a strategic location or create play and learning areas for children. This technology also gives the ability to clearly mark exits or draw attention to merchandise.

Custom Logo Colors

With over 25 color options to choose from, you can achieve looks that are bold and daring or subtle and refined. All of our solid EPDM colors contain approximately 18 per cent postindustrial content. Available for Square-cut and Interlocking tiles.
