Community Involvement

We are proud to work with various charities and organizations active in the community who lend support and services to various groups in need. We believe that it is essential for businesses to be actively involved to foster a strong community for everyone.

Salmon Arm Fish & Game Club

Salmon Arm Fish & Game Club strives to ensure the sound, long term management of fish, wildlife and outdoor recreational resources; this includes making the general public aware of the dangers of land, air and water pollution and to cooperate with other groups with similar objectives. In addition they also promote true appreciation for sportsmanship and sports safety in light of current fish and wildlife regulations.

MS Society

The MS Society provides services to people with multiple sclerosis and their families. They also fund research to find the cause and cure for this disease. They have a membership of 17,000 and are the only national voluntary organization in Canada that supports both MS research and services. Since their founding in 1948, the core support of the MS Society has been from the thousands of dedicated individuals, companies and foundations in communities across Canada.

Shuswap Family Resource & Referral Society

The Shuswap Family Resource & Referral Society offers many programs to support various social issues. From adoption resource and support to sustainable food and abuse intervention programs. Their Philosophy is to support families and individuals of all ages, cultures, and socio-economic backgrounds. They provide programs delivered individually or in groups as well as providing a referral service to other services found in the community of Salmon Arm and surrounding areas. They work with individual and family strengths to encourage personal growth, wellness, and positive change.

Canadian Cancer Society

The Canadian Cancer Society is a national, community-based organization whose mission is the eradication of cancer and the enhancement of the quality of life of people living with cancer.

Vancouver General Hospital Prostate Cancer Research

Prostate cancer is a leading cancer in men and the second leading cause of cancer deaths. By understanding the reasons for treatment resistance in advanced prostate cancer and translating that into new products and treatments, the Vancouver Prostate Centre is focused on improving longevity and quality of life for patients in BC, and around the world.

Safe Society Women's Shelter

The Shuswap Area Family Emergency Society (SAFE) was established in 1979 by a group of women in the community who recognized the need for a women's shelter/safe haven for women experiencing violence in the home.

Kidsport

KidSport believes that no kid should be left on the sidelines and all should be given the opportunity to experience the positive benefits of organized sports. 1 in 3 kids can't afford the cost of organized sport. KidSport is a national non-profit organization that provides support to children in order to remove financial barriers that prevent them from playing organized sports.

Children's Day Festival

The purpose of the Shuswap Hospital Foundation is to accept, manage and disburse funds and properties to the benefit of Shuswap Health Services. This entity includes Shuswap Lake General Hospital, Bastion Place, (a residential care facility) and Community Care Services. Funds are used to help meet the need for priority equipment and facilities that will enhance the care of patients and residents of these health care facilities and services

R.J. Haney Heritage Village and Museum

R.J. Haney Heritage Village and Museum is a 40-acre park operated by the Salmon Arm Museum and Heritage Association. Their Mission is to keep alive the heritage and history of Salmon Arm and surrounding area by collecting, preserving, documenting and interpreting artifacts, records and stories of the region in a public community museum.

Shuswap Daycare Society

Shuswap Daycare Society is a non-profit organization offering quality daycare to children and families from Salmon Arm and area. They have various programs to meet the specific requirements of children.

Shane Gorner Endowment Fund

The family of Shane Gorner established a memorial scholarship fund with the Shuswap Community Foundation. On an annual basis the Shane Gorner Memorial Bursary is given to a Grade 12 student attending Salmon Arm Senior Secondary School who:

  • demonstrates talent and achievement in the arts, particularly the performing arts, visual arts or music
  • is a person with strong academic ability although not necessarily a top academic achiever
  • intends to pursue post-secondary studies or training in the arts or in a field related to the arts
  • is a person of good character and who exhibits Shane's qualities of optimism, maturity and dedication to his artistic pursuits
  • has need of financial assistance
