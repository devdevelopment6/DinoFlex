TECHNICAL

STRIDE FITNESS TILES – CLEANING TIPS

When you receive your order of Dinoflex Stride Fitness tiles, they will appear slightly more slippery than Sport Mat or Evolution Tiles. This is because a release agent is used as part of the manufacturing process. This will wear away with use, but for fast removal clean with a solution of hot water and a mild neutral cleaner. Be sure to mop with clean water to rinse away the cleaning solution.

In rare circumstances, a more aggressive cleaning may be required to remove the release agent. This can typically be accomplished using a water-based cleaner such as Stoner A538, which has a high pH. The high pH helps emulsify the release agent while detergents keep it in a suspension allowing it to be rinsed away. The undiluted A538 should be applied to the tiles with a microfiber mop and let stand for 15 minutes.  The cleaned area should be rinsed using a rotary scrubber with a cylindrical brush, and warm, clean water to remove residual cleaner. The water must be extracted after this step to remove any remaining moisture. Once the Stride Fitness Tiles are completely dry, the surface should have a higher traction.
